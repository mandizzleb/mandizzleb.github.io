Thursday is the second mixtape by Canadian singer The Weeknd and was independently released on August 18, 2011. Its music incorporates downtempo, dubstep, dream pop, hip hop, rock and reggae styles and was praised for the innovative direction in took following the criticially acclaimed House of Balloons. As with his previous works, Canadian record producers Doc McKinney and Illangelo were responsible for production of the mixtape. Fellow Canadian artist Drake is the sole feature on the project one of one the best Weeknd songs of all time "The Zone". There are a number of gems on this project which are highly regarded by critics and fans alike and here I'll be reviewing them.
